Frequently Asked Questions Get answers to all your doubts

How often can you give the test?

The student can only appear the test once.

What is the test requirement?

The applicant needs to login and register himself/herself before attending the test.

What qualifications do you need to give the test?

You should either be a school student, a graduate or a dropout.

Can anyone take the test?

We have provided three levels of test - one for school students, the other for graduates, and the last one for the dropouts. Therefore, anyone within these three levels can take the test.

Are the questions valid, would it help the student anyway?

Yes, the test questions are verified by a certified psychometric analyst. So, the test would be absolutely helpful.

Is this a paid career counselling?

No, initially we are providing free career counselling to the students of these three levels. Hence, the student of any of these three levels can appear the test.

Is it an online or offline?

The test is available both online and offline.

What topic does the questionnaire cover?

The test mostly covers six fields of interest based upon the applicant’s personality and their level of the test.

What is the duration of the test?

For each level the test is for 90 minutes.
